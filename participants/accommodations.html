Accommodation Information

Sandman Hotel Lethbridge

For accommodation in Lethbridge we recommend the Sandman Hotel Lethbridge which also has practical access to public transportation.

Spaces are limited, therefore reserve your room by November 2nd, 2023 to guarantee the discounted conference rate, by calling this number: 403-328-1111 or 1-800-726-3626 / 1-800-SANDMAN.

Standard rooms for two queen: $99.00 + tax per night (Continental breakfast is NOT included in the price)

In order to receive the correct rates, callers must reference:

  • Sandman Hotel Lethbridge
  • Group Name: Alberta Conference on Linguistics
  • Block ID: #325681
  • Block code: ABLING23

A valid credit card is required at the time of reservation to secure any guests rooms.

Ramada by Wyndham

We also recommend the Ramada by Wyndham which also has practical access to public transportation.

Reserve your room by November 7th, 2023 to guarantee the discounted conference rate, by calling this number: (403) 329-0258. You will need to mention you are a participating at the Alberta Conference On Linguistics hosted at the University of Lethbridge.

Standard 1 Queen Bedrooms: $114 plus taxes / per room / for one night

Double Bedrooms: $114 plus taxes / per room / for one night

Continental breakfast is included in the price

Sandman Signature Lethbridge Lodge

The Sandman Signature has also offered a discount for individuals participating in the Alberta Conference On Linguistics which is closer to the University and has a bus stop right in front of the hotel.

Spaces are once again limited, therefore reserve your room by November 2nd, 2023 to guarantee the discounted conference rate, by calling this number: 403-328-1123 or 1-800-726-3626 / 1-800-SANDMAN.

Corporate 2 Queen or Corporate King: $139.00 + tax per room per night for the 2 queen (Continental breakfast is NOT included in the price)

In order to receive the correct rates, callers must reference:

  • Sandman Signature Lethbridge Lodge
  • Group Name: Alberta Conference on Linguistics
  • Block ID: #325679
  • Block code: ALBERTALING

A valid credit card is required at the time of reservation to secure any guests rooms.
